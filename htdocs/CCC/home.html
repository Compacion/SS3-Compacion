Introducing Calorie Calculator

By: Cristy C. Compacion


A calorie calculator is a tool that helps estimate the number of calories a person should consume in a day,

based on factors like weight and activity level. By understanding your calorie needs,

you can make informed decisions about your diet and exercise routine.



BMR

Basal Metabolic Rate: This is the number of calories your body needs to maintain basic functions at rest.

TDEE

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE): This adjusts the BMR based on your activity level.

Adjusting

Consume as many calories as you burn or eating fewer calories than you burn.
